33 POOL SETUP — PACKAGE 6
Firebase Payment Ledger

Player features:
- Real Firebase payment balance.
- $54 season total.
- Amount due through the current NFL week.
- Current/behind prize-payment eligibility.
- Payment and adjustment history.
- Winnings summary prepared for the scoring package.

Commissioner features:
- Claimed-player payment board.
- Search by player name or schedule number.
- Record cash, check, Venmo, PayPal, or other payments.
- Record refunds and negative corrections.
- Firestore transaction updates summary, transaction history, and audit log together.
- Original transaction records cannot be edited or deleted by the app.
- Visible Firebase refresh confirmation.

Firestore collections added:
- payments
- paymentTransactions
- audit entries for payment changes
