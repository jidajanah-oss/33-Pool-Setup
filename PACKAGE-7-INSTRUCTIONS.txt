33 POOL SETUP — PACKAGE 7
Firebase Weekly Scoring, Rolling Pot, and Payout Records

Adds:
- Commissioner final-score entry for all 32 NFL teams.
- Official NFL bye teams disabled automatically for each week.
- Draft score saving in Firestore.
- Exact final-score-of-33 winner detection.
- Multiple exact-33 winners split the complete pot.
- No-winner carryover plus the next $96 weekly addition.
- Week 18 closest-to-33 resolution, over or under.
- Multiple equally close Week 18 teams split the pot.
- Penny remainders are distributed one cent at a time by schedule number so
  the full pot is always awarded.
- Winner records and earned amounts added to Firebase payment accounts.
- Prize status: pending, on hold when not paid through the winning week, or paid.
- Commissioner Mark Paid control.
- Reopen only the most recently finalized week before a prize is paid.
- Player Weekly Board final scores and winner highlights.
- Player Pot Tracker with finalized weekly history.
- Next week's public assignments publish automatically after finalization.
- Opponents remain hidden everywhere.

Important:
Package 7 requires all 32 schedule numbers to be claimed before an official
week can be finalized. Scores may be entered and saved before that.

Install:
1. Copy the package files into:
   C:\Users\jidaj\Documents\GitHub\33-Pool-Setup
2. Publish FIREBASE-PACKAGE-7-RULES.txt in Firestore Rules.
3. Run:
   npm run build
   npm run dev

Safe test:
- Enter a few Week 1 scores and click Save Draft Scores.
- Confirm the Weekly Board displays those saved final scores.
- Official Finalize remains disabled until all 32 players have claimed.
- Do not use fake final scores on the live season unless you intend to keep
  them; draft scores can be edited before finalization.
