33 POOL SETUP — PACKAGE 10
Player Invitations + Commissioner Team + NFL Status Cleanup

Adds:
- Removes raw ESPN values such as STATUS_SCHEDULED from the commissioner score cards.
- Cleans already-saved Firebase status text immediately.
- Commissioner invitation form that sends a Firebase email sign-in link.
- Invitation tracking with Invite Pending and Signed In status.
- Resend invitation control.
- Invited player name is used when the Firebase profile is first created.
- One protected Primary Commissioner.
- Two controlled Backup Commissioner slots.
- Only the Primary Commissioner may assign, replace, or remove backups.
- Backup Commissioners retain normal commissioner controls and may invite players.
- Commissioner-role changes use a Firestore transaction and audit record.
- PWA cache advances to production v12.

Important workflow:
1. Send an invitation.
2. The person opens the email link and signs in once.
3. Their account appears in the signed-in user dropdown.
4. The Primary Commissioner assigns them to Backup 1 or Backup 2.
5. The new backup should refresh or sign out and back in to load commissioner access.
